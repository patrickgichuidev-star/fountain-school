About Us

Our Heritage

Founded in 2010 with a Vision for Excellence

Fountain Group of Schools – Tigoni was established to provide world‑class education in the serene highlands of Kiambu County. Located in Murengeti, Tigoni, Limuru, the school has grown into a centre of academic and personal excellence.

Mission: To empower learners with knowledge, skills and values for lifelong success.

Vision: A leading school producing holistic, confident and innovative leaders.

Motto: "Empowering Every Learner to Excel"

Welcome to Fountain
Welcome Message

A Place Where Every Child Thrives

Welcome to Fountain Group of Schools – Tigoni, a vibrant learning community nestled in the serene environment of Murengeti, Tigoni, Limuru. Since our founding in 2010, we have been dedicated to providing a transformative education that empowers every learner to discover their unique potential.

Our school offers the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) from Playgroup through Grade 9, supported by modern facilities including ICT centres, science laboratories, a well-stocked library, and spacious sports grounds. We believe in nurturing the whole child—academically, socially, physically, and spiritually.

Why Choose Fountain

What Makes Us Exceptional

Discover the Fountain difference—where tradition meets innovation in a nurturing environment.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous CBC curriculum delivered by dedicated educators in small class sizes, ensuring personalized attention and outstanding learning outcomes.

Technology Integration

Digital classrooms and ICT centres equip students with 21st-century skills, preparing them for a technology-driven future.

Character Development

Partnerships with Scripture Union and Go Kids International foster spiritual growth, leadership, and strong moral values.

Modern Boarding

Safe, comfortable dormitories with warm water, 24/7 supervision, and a home-like atmosphere for boarding students.

Holistic Development

Over 20 co-curricular activities including sports, music, drama, chess, debate, and educational trips nurture well-rounded individuals.

Safe Environment

Secure campus with comprehensive security measures, health services, and a supportive community that prioritizes student wellbeing.
